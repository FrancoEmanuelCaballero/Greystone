About us?

We are determined public accountants. For this reason, we want Greystone to be recognized at the national level, not only for being service providers in accounting matters, but also for being the best allies for your business or company, because it satisfies us to grow hand in hand with our clients.

Given the constant changes in tax reforms, Greystone is born, a group of fully professional public accountants, who share as a labor mission the firm conviction of offering effective solutions and accounting and tax advice to each of our clients, under the guarantee of receiving a personalized and specialized attention.

Our experience in the accounting field has given us the ability to facilitate relationships that involve the treasury and taxpayers. Our quality, honesty, confidentiality, integrity and constancy have led us to maintain a committed and transparent line of work.

As part of our accounting services, we can offer you the following solutions:

  • Design and general organization of the accounting procedure
  • Issuance of invoices
  • Payment management
  • Accounts receivable management
  • Preparation of monthly and annual reports
  • Cash flow
  • Accounting supervision
  • Accounting under IFRS or US GAAP standards
  • Preparation of annual Financial Statements
  • Attention to tax inspections on accounting records and books
  • File and maintenance of accounting books
